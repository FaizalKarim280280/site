Projects

RoadMent

RoadMent is an end-to-end platform for segmentation of roads from satellite images. This is a project based on segmenting out roads from satellite images. We have used the U-Net architecture for the model, coupled with dice coefficient loss for loss function and IoU score and dice coefficient as metrics.

After model training, we got a training IoU score of 0.497 and a validation IoU score of 0.415. Finally, the model was integrated on a website using the Flask API.   [Code]

Clean/Dirty Road Classification

This is a project based on ConvNets used to identify whether a road is clean or dirty. We have used MobileNet as our base architecture and the weights are based on imagenet. We have used web scraping techniques to download 238 images of both clean and dirty roads.

We achieved a training accuracy of 97.1% and validation accuracy of 91.6%.   [Code]

Flood Segmentation

The aim of this project was to build a deep learning model which will identify and segment flooded areas in aerial images. For this project, we have built our own custom dataset from Google Images. The dataset contains 290 aerial images of several flooded and their respective masks. Both the masks and the images are 224x224 in resolution and are present in .png format.

After training for 15 epochs, we obtained a training iou score of 0.79 and validation iou score of 0.71.   [Code]
